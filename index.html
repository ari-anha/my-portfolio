About Me

Hello! I'm Arianha.

Thank you for visiting my page! I'm currently studying a part-time software engineer course at Manchester Codes. It's hard work, but I'm really enjoying it!

After working as a Japanese & English bilingual administrator for over 5 years, I wanted to move into an industry which could take me pretty much anywhere in the world, as one of my future dreams is to live in Berlin. Some of my friends who were already in the industry recommended that I try out coding, and so I decided to dive into it!

When I'm not practicing coding, you can usually find me in front of the sewing machine stitching away my latest sewing project, which is likely to be a vintage reproduction from the 1930s or 40s. I also enjoy cooking with family and friends. Wrapping dumplings and rolling sushi is really fun when there's loads of hands to help!
